## System Requirements

### Minimum
- **OS:** Windows 10+, macOS 11+, Ubuntu 20.04+
- **RAM:** 4GB
- **CPU:** 2 cores
- **Disk:** 5GB free space

### Recommended
- **RAM:** 8GB+ (for self-hosted AI)
- **CPU:** 4+ cores
- **Disk:** 10GB+ free space
